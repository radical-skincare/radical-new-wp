Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a cost to join?

No. Applying and participating in the Radical Brand Partner Program is free.

Do I need to be approved?

Yes. All Brand Partner applications are reviewed prior to acceptance into the program.

How are sales tracked?

Sales are tracked through your unique Brand Partner link and customer code.

When are commissions paid?

Commissions are paid monthly once a minimum balance of $100 in earned commissions has been reached. Commission balances below $100 will roll over to the following month until the payout threshold is met.

What commission rate do Brand Partners earn?

Approved Brand Partners earn 10% commission on qualifying sales generated through their unique Brand Partner link or customer code.

Can anyone become a Brand Partner?

We welcome applications from individuals and businesses whose audience, content, and values align with the Radical Skincare brand.

Brand Partner Terms

Participation in the Radical Brand Partner Program is subject to approval and ongoing compliance with program policies. Radical Skincare reserves the right to approve, deny, suspend, or terminate any Brand Partner account at its sole discretion.

Participation in the program does not create an employment, agency, franchise, or business ownership relationship with Radical Skincare.
